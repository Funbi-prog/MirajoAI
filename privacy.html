Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18th, 2025

At Mirajo Agency, accessible from www.mirajoagency.org, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

We may collect personal information from you when you visit our site, register, subscribe, respond to a survey, or fill out a form. This includes:

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Other information you provide us

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Subcontractors may access data only for support services. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process transactions

How We Protect Your Information

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal info. We may share it with trusted service providers who help operate our website or support you, provided they agree to confidentiality terms.

Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience. You may set your browser to reject cookies, but some parts of the site may not work properly.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review it periodically.
